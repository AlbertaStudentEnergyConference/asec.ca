The Alberta Student Energy Conference is building the future for Canada. We are a step for the energy industry, connecting knowledgeable professionals with ambitious students who will inherit and progress Canada's Energy. Reinventing existing technology and developing new methodologies, ASEC is building momentum.

ASEC aims to introduce companies to the students who can bring fresh ideas and motivation to an industry of opportunities. This is achieved through discussion, presentation, competition, and collaboration.
